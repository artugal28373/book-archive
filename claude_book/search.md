## How to use this list

1. Open any line, copy it, paste into your search engine of choice
2. Look for the **author's official site or publisher's official site first** — many books have free legal versions
3. For paid books, check (in this order): your university library → O'Reilly Learning subscription → Humble Bundle archive → publisher direct → Kindle (regional pricing) → physical bookstore

Stars (⭐) in `BOOKS-TO-DOWNLOAD.md` indicate the highest-priority books per category.
